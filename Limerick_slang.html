10 LIMERICK SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Tackies

Meaning: Tracksuits

Example: "No way am I jumping that wall, these tackies cost me a fortune!"



#2. Beour

Meaning: An attractive woman, thought to originate from "beautiful"

Example: "C'mere I must go, I'm meeting me beour in town"



#3. Daycent

Meaning: It's great!

Example: "Aw sure it's daycent enough"



#4. Fair Daycent

Meaning: Quite excellent!

Example: "This grub is fair daycent"



#5. Pure Daycent

Meaning: Incredible!

Example: "This place is pure daycent"



#6. UNREAL

Meaning: Pronounced "unrale", this means the most sublime thing which can exist

Example: "That new Honda Civic that Pa's after getting is unreal"



#7. Aboy the kid

Meaning: Well done, congratulations or just a friendly compliment

Example: "You got the job! Aboy the kid"



#8. Ghoulbag

Meaning: An unpleasant person

Example: "Ah kid, can't face work on Monday, shower of ghoulbags in that office"



#9. Peel an orange in his pocket

Meaning: Someone stingy, or mean (pronounced mane)

Example: Kid 1: "That ghoulbag is after dodging his round again"

Kid 2: "Sure what d'ya expect, he'd peel an orange in his pocket that lad"



#10. Put the heart crossways in you

Meaning: To give a thoroughly unpleasnt shock

Example: Pa drives past loudly his new Subaru

Biddy 1: "Christ almighty theres some hum of that car"

Biddy 2: "Sure jaysus it has the heart put crossways in me!"
