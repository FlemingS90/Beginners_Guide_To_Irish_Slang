10 BELFAST SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Talent

Meaning: Good looking females/males

Example: "She has a lot of talent"



#2. Canny

Meaning: Cannot

Example: "I canny go out tonight"



#3. Critter

Meaning: Used to describe someone that you have pity for

Example: "Look at him out in the rain, the poor critter"



#4. Belly up

Meaning: Unsuccessful

Example: "Boy: How's the new business going?""

Girl: "Terrible, it's gone belly up already"



#5. Swallied/Half-cut / Cut / Full

Meaning: To be drunk

Example: "I was seriously swallied last night"

Boy 1: "I'm starting to feel a bit cut lads"

Boy 2: "Feeling half-cut myself"

Boy 3: "Me too lads, starting to get full"



#6. Ride

Meaning: Attractive female or male

Example: "He's such a ride"



#7. Swally

Meaning: A drink

Example: "Do you wanna go for a swally after work?"



#8. See

Meaning: Engage in a relationship with the opposite sex

Example: Girl 1: "You're gonig out with him?"

Girl 2: "Yea, we've been seeing each other for a week now"



#9. Away on!

Meaning: You must be joking!

Example: Boy: "You'll never guess who I just met...David Beckham!"

Man: "Away on with ye!"



#10. What about ye?

Meaning: As in how are you doing my good man

Example: "Yeah, yeah I'm doing well, what about ye?"
