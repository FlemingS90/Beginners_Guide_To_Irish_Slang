10 CORK SLANG WORDS AND PHRASES EXPLAINED IN ENGLISH

#1. Mint

Meaning: Cool or great

Example: "Aw cheers for the present, it's mint"



#2. Bazzer

Meaning: Haircut

Example: "Christ will you look at the bazzer on your man!"



#3. Gat

Meaning: An alcoholic beverage

Example: "Going gatting with the boys Tuesday night, gonna be mint!"



#4. Doing a line

Meaning: Maintaining a steady and commited relationship

Example: "No surprise they're engaged, sure they've been doing a line since they shifted in third class!"



#5. Langer

Meaning: A complete idiot

Example: "He's an absolute langer"



#6. Langers

Meaning: To be intoxicated

Example: "He was so langers he couldn't even open his eyes to find his pint"



#7. Haunted

Meaning: Lucky or fortunate

Example: Lad 1: "Hust after winning a fiver on a scratchcard!"

Lad 2: "Ah you're haunted, that's the second time this month!"



#8. Flah

Meaning: An attractive person

Example: Jimmy: "God that Emma Watson is an absolute flah"



#9. Out with me

Meaning: To be annoyed at or ignoring someone

Example: Boy 1: "Why didn't Sophie say hello back there?"

Boy 2: "Ah she's been out with me since I got langers at the weekend and drunk texted her"



#10. Jag

Meaning: To go on a date

Example: Sister: "Where's Sean gone?"

Mother: "He's gone to meet a girl! Isn't it great?"

Sister: "Jaysus never thought my little brother would be off on a jag. Hashtag forever alone"
